Introduction & Pronunciation Guide

Welcome to your Easy Spanish Phrase Book!

This comprehensive guide contains over 700 essential Spanish phrases organized by topic to help you communicate effectively in Spanish-speaking countries. Each phrase includes the English translation, Spanish text, and approximate pronunciation guide.

Basic Pronunciation Rules

A
like 'a' in "father"
ah
E
like 'e' in "bet"
eh
I
like 'ee' in "meet"
ee
O
like 'o' in "note"
oh
U
like 'oo' in "boot"
oo
Note: The pronunciation guide uses English sounds as approximations. For best results, listen to native speakers whenever possible.
