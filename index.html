WEIGHT LOSS SUCCESS GUIDELINES
Guidelines

💧 HYDRATION

  • Drink 16oz water before each workout
  • Sip during exercise every 15-20 minutes
  • Aim for 3-4 liters total daily
  • Add lemon or cucumber for flavor

🍎 NUTRITION TIMING

  • Pre-workout: Light carbs (banana)
  • Post-workout: Protein within 30min
  • Eat every 3-4 hours
  • Stop eating 3 hours before bed

📊 TRACKING PROGRESS

  • Weigh weekly, same time/day
  • Take measurements every 2 weeks
  • Progress photos monthly
  • Track workout intensity & duration
